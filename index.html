What's Acai?

Acai is a berry that grows in the Amazon Rainforest. It is a superfood that is high in antioxidants, fiber, and healthy fats. It is also low in sugar and high in protein. Acai is a great source of energy and is a great way to start your day. We harvest these berries and turn them into a paste. This is like a purple, delicious, healthy ice-cream. Add some fruits and nuts and you have youself an Acai Bowl, good for breakfast or a snack on the go.
