What's Yo Yetti?

Yo Yetti is a place where you can get a healthy, delicious, and fun meal. We have a variety of Acai Bowls and Froyo. To maximize family fun, we also have a variety of trivia and conversation starters created by the community. A delicious bowl with family fun is the perfect way to cool down on a hot day.

What's Acai?

Acai is a berry that grows in the Amazon Rainforest. It is a superfood that is high in antioxidants, fiber, and healthy fats. It is also low in sugar and high in protein. Acai is a great source of energy and is a great way to start your day. We harvest these berries and turn them into a paste. This is like a purple, delicious, healthy ice-cream. Add some fruits and nuts and you have youself an Acai Bowl, good for breakfast or a snack on the go.

Family & community

Phones and TV completely ruin the family experience. That's why Yo Yetti's menu comes with conversation starters and trivia. Answer all of the trivia correct for free Acai Bowl/Froyo for the whole family! You can even upload your own (family friendly). A yum, fun, family experience is the perfect community project.
